Places to Visit in Trivandrum

Explore Trivandrum! - coastal city blending heritage, serene beaches, vibrant culture, ancient temples, lush landscapes, and captivating tourist attractions.

Sri Padmanabhaswamy Temple is an ancient Hindu temple in Thiruvananthapuram, renowned for its Dravidian architecture, spiritual significance, and historical legacy dedicated to Lord Vishnu.

Attukal Bhagavathi Temple in Thiruvananthapuram is renowned for the Attukal Pongala, the worldâ€™s largest women-led religious gathering, celebrating devotion, faith, and cultural unity.

Aazhimala Siva Temple is a scenic coastal temple near Thiruvananthapuram, famous for its massive Shiva statue overlooking the Arabian Sea and its calm spiritual atmosphere.

Beemapally Masjid, located in Thiruvananthapuram, Kerala, is a sprawling mosque complex renowned for its traditional ornate architecture and spiritual significance.

St. Mary's Malankara Syrian Catholic Major Archieparchial Cathedral in Pattom, Thiruvananthapuram, is a stunning stone-built church over 70 years old, blending traditional architecture with modern touches.

Pazhavangadi Ganapathy Temple is a prominent temple dedicated to Lord Ganesha, located in the bustling city center of Thiruvananthapuram, Kerala. The temple is known for its unique idol of Lord Ganesha in the seated posture with his right knee folded.
