Places to Visit in Trivandrum

Explore Trivandrum! - coastal city blending heritage, serene beaches, vibrant culture, ancient temples, lush landscapes, and captivating tourist attractions.

MUSEUMS

Kuthiramalika Palace Museum

Built by Swathi Thirunal Rama Varma, this 18th-century palace features 122 carved teak horses and intricate wooden ceilings. A masterpiece of Kerala architecture using rosewood and granite, it offers a rare glimpse into royal life of Travancore. Nearby attractions include the Sree Padmanabhaswamy Temple and the iconic Methan Mani clock tower.

Napier Museum

Napier museum features historic coins, musical instruments, and intricate bronze sculptures of Hindu Gods. Its vast collection spans ancient dynasties, from the Cholas to the Romans. Nearby attractions include the Sree Chitra and Raja Ravi Varma galleries, Trivandrum Zoo, and Kanakakunnu Palace.

Sree Chitra Art Gallery

Located near Napier Museum, this gallery features 1,100 masterpieces, including Mughal, Rajput, and Oriental art. It showcases unique Tibetan Thangkas, Balinese paintings, and historic Indian murals. The adjacent Raja Ravi Varma Gallery displays world-famous works. It is conveniently located within the Napier Museum grounds near the Trivandrum Zoo.

Kerala Science and Technology Museum

This interactive hub features hands-on exhibits like the "Infinite Well" and "Nail Chair," alongside life-sized scientific artifacts. The complex includes the Priyadarshini Planetarium, a 3D theater, and a space gallery with spacecraft replicas, making science engaging for visitors of all ages.

Natural History Museum

The museum offers an impressive display of the animal kingdom, featuring from tiny ants to majestic whales. Kerala Legislative Museum

The G. Karthikeyan Legislature Museum, located behind the Kerala Legislative Assembly in Thiruvananthapuram, is a two-story facility documenting the state's political history. It features three galleries, interactive kiosks, photographs of chief ministers, and a dedicated Mahatma Gandhi gallery, highlighting the evolution of Kerala's democratic, legislative assembly.

Vikram Sarabhai Space Centre

Vikram Sarabhai Space Centre (VSSC), Thiruvananthapuram, is the lead centre of ISRO responsible for the design and development of launch vehicle technology. The Centre pursues active research and development and undertakes crucial responsibilities of design, manufacturing, analysis, development and testing related to the realisation of subsystems for the different missions.

Sunil's Wax Museum

Pearl Art Gallery in Thiruvananthapuram specializes in handcrafted, premium shell pearl, and gemstone stainless steel jewelry. Offering a blend of modern elegance and traditional design, this boutique showcases unique, artisanal pieces.

Kanakakkunnu Palace

This hill-top palace, built by the Travancore kings, is a premier cultural hub famous for its colonial-style architecture and vast green lawns. It serves as a stunning venue for international festivals, open-air concerts, and the annual grand illumination during the Onam season.
